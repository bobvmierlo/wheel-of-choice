          🔗 Share

          This code belongs to this wheel only — your other wheels stay private. Give it to your partner or the whole group: everyone who joins with it spins this same wheel, with its entries, favourites and history in sync, while each of you keeps their own filters.

          Even easier: send them this invite link. Creating an account through it joins this wheel automatically.

          🔔 Notifications

          Get a ping on this device when something happens on one of your wheels — someone spins and waits for your thumbs-up, a pick gets vetoed, the decision is final, or a member adds a new entry. Works even when the app is closed; each device that should buzz turns this on for itself.

          📲 On an iPhone or iPad, notifications only work once the app is on your Home Screen (iOS 16.4 or newer):

          1. Open this site in Safari
          2. Tap the Share button
          3. Choose Add to Home Screen
          4. Open the 🎡 app from your Home Screen and tap 🔔 again

              🔑 Change password

              Pick a new password for your account. You'll stay logged in on this device; other devices keep their session too.

              🛠️ Admin

              Every account on this server. Hand out admin rights, pull someone out of all the wheels they share with others (they keep their account and any wheels of their own), or delete an account for good.

                Registration

                When registration is open, anyone can create an account. On an internet-facing server you'll usually want it closed, and to invite people one link at a time. (Wheel invite links keep working either way — sharing a wheel always lets the newcomer sign up.)

                Invite links each let one person create an account, then stop working. Hand them out when registration is closed.

                  Date polls

                  Tune the restaurant date polls for everyone on this server. Changes apply to new and in-progress polls straight away.

                  Backup

                  Everything — accounts, wheels, history — lives in one db.json on the server. Download a copy now and then; restoring one replaces all current data.

                  Server

                  Fetch the latest version from git and restart the server. Needs the updater units from deploy/ installed.
